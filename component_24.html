14-Day Namibia Self-Drive

Comfort Safari

Overview

This exclusive Namibian journey takes you through the country's most iconic landscapes, blending adventure and relaxation at every turn. From the red dunes of the Kalahari to the coastal charm of Swakopmund, you'll experience the thrill of exploring ancient deserts, witnessing wildlife in Etosha, and uncovering cultural gems like Damaraland's rock art.

Itinerary

Day 1 - 2: Ti Melen Boutique Guesthouse

Experience the charm of Windhoek in a boutique guesthouse blending modern comforts with local warmth. Spend your first night in Namibia surrounded by the intimate atmosphere of Ti Melen, ideal for a peaceful start to your adventure.

Lodge: Ti Melen Boutique Guesthouse

Location: Windhoek

Day 2 - 3: Kalahari Anib Lodge

Discover the beauty of the Kalahari at this eco-friendly lodge. The towering red dunes and starry nights set the perfect backdrop for a romantic evening. Enjoy delicious cuisine and desert exploration before retreating to your cozy room.

Lodge: Kalahari Anib Lodge

Location: Kalahari Desert

Day 3 - 5: Desert Homestead Lodge

Immerse yourself in the surreal beauty of the Namib Desert. Stay at Desert Homestead, nestled between the red dunes of Sossusvlei, where you can explore the world's tallest dunes and return to luxury at the lodge for intimate sunset views.

Lodge: Desert Homestead Lodge

Location: Sossusvlei

Day 5 - 7: Swakopmund Guesthouse

Blend adventure with relaxation at this charming guesthouse in Swakopmund, offering easy access to the beach and colonial-style comforts. Explore the unique coastal town with its German heritage, or unwind with a stroll along the ocean.

Lodge: Swakopmund Guesthouse

Location: Swakopmund

Day 7 - 9: Lodge Damaraland

In the heart of Damaraland, experience timeless landscapes and ancient rock art. Stay in a comfortable lodge designed to blend with its surroundings, offering exciting excursions to explore the desert's hidden gems, including desert elephants.

Lodge: Lodge Damaraland

Location: Damaraland

Day 9 - 10: Etosha Safari Camp

Set near the southern entrance to Etosha National Park, Etosha Safari Camp gives you access to one of Africa's most celebrated wildlife sanctuaries. Enjoy an evening of delicious meals and sunset views before heading into the park for safari adventures.

Lodge: Etosha Safari Camp

Location: Etosha National Park

Day 10 - 12: Onguma Bush Camp

Enjoy luxurious tented suites at Onguma, a private reserve bordering Etosha National Park. The tranquil setting allows for both thrilling game drives and romantic evenings. Unwind by the campfire after an exciting day of wildlife watching.

Lodge: Onguma Bush Camp

Location: Etosha Border Reserve

Day 12 - 14: Otjiwa Collection - Eagle's Rest

End your Namibian adventure in luxury at Eagle's Rest. Nestled in the peaceful Otjiwa Nature Reserve, the lodge offers exceptional service and the chance to unwind with private bush walks, spa treatments, or simply relax in your charming accommodation.

Lodge: Otjiwa Collection - Eagle's Rest

Location: Otjiwa Nature Reserve
