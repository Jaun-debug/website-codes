01 What is a self-drive safari?

A self-drive safari gives you the freedom to explore Africa's wild spaces entirely at your own pace. You travel in your own rental vehicle, stop for photos whenever something extraordinary catches your eye, and experience the open road with nature as your only guide.

There are no rigid schedules, no group dynamics to navigate — just you, the landscape, and Namibia's extraordinary wildlife unfolding at every turn.

Sossusvlei, Namibia

02 Do I need a 4x4 vehicle?

For most Namibian routes, a 4x4 is strongly recommended. Gravel roads, sandy tracks, and remote river crossings are beautifully driveable in a properly equipped 4x4 — and far riskier in a standard sedan.

A 4x4 also opens up more remote parks, dramatic viewpoints, and off-the-beaten-path destinations that most travellers simply never reach.

Damaraland, Namibia
