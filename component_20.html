18-Day Hidden Treasures of Namibia

Self-Drive Safari

Overview

Embark on an unforgettable 18-day tour of Namibia, a country known for its breathtaking landscapes and rich wildlife. Your adventure starts in the capital, Windhoek, where you'll enjoy a stay at the cozy Ti Melen Boutique Guesthouse. From there, travel through the vast Kalahari Desert and the awe-inspiring Fish River Canyon, experiencing the unique desert scenery. Along the way, you'll stay at top lodges like the Desert Homestead Lodge in Sossusvlei, where you can explore the towering sand dunes, and Alte Villa in Luderitz, a peaceful retreat surrounded by nature.

The tour continues along Namibia's coastline, where you'll visit Swakopmund and take in the stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean. You'll also discover the beauty of Epupa Falls and get up close to the wildlife in Etosha National Park. With stays at Opuwo Country Lodge and Hobatere Lodge, you'll experience Namibia's diverse ecosystems, from deserts to lush landscapes. This tour offers the perfect mix of adventure and relaxation.

Start your journey at the welcoming Ti Melen Boutique Guesthouse in Windhoek. This charming spot offers a comfortable stay, perfect for relaxing after your travels. With its cozy atmosphere and personalized service, it's a great way to ease into Namibia's unique charm before the adventure begins.

Day 2-3: Kalahari Anib Lodge

Next, head to Kalahari Anib Lodge in the heart of the Kalahari Desert. Day 3-4: Canyon Roadhouse

After the Kalahari, journey to the stunning Fish River Canyon and stay at the Canyon Roadhouse. This rustic lodge, with its cool, vintage style, is the perfect base to discover one of the world's largest canyons, known for its striking landscapes and natural wonders. The lodge's laid-back atmosphere and charming décor make it an ideal place to relax and take in the panoramic views of the canyon.
