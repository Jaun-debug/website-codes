Frequently Asked Questions

What is a self-drive safari?

A self-drive safari gives you the freedom to explore Africa's wild spaces at your own pace. You travel in your own rental vehicle, stop for photos whenever you like, and enjoy the adventure of the open road with nature as your guide.

Do I need a 4x4 vehicle?

For most routes, a 4x4 is recommended to handle gravel roads and sandy tracks comfortably. It also gives you access to more remote parks and viewpoints, making your safari experience far richer and more rewarding.
