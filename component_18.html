11-Day Wildlife of Namibia

Self-Drive Safari

Overview

This 11-day tour takes you through Namibia's diverse landscapes, offering a mix of luxury, adventure, and wildlife. Start in Windhoek at Ti Melen Boutique Guesthouse, then head to Le Mirage Resort & Spa near Sossusvlei to explore the famous dunes. After that, visit Swakopmund Luxury Suites, where you can enjoy beachside charm and adventure activities like dune boarding. Your journey continues with Twyfelfontein Adventure Camp, where you'll discover ancient rock engravings and the beauty of Damaraland.

The tour moves on to Okutala Etosha Lodge for thrilling game drives and wildlife encounters in Etosha National Park. Finally, relax at Waterberg Plateau Lodge, set in the lush Waterberg Plateau National Park, offering stunning views and a chance to explore the park's rich wildlife. This carefully designed itinerary provides the perfect balance of relaxation and adventure across Namibia's most iconic destinations.
