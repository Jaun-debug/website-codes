17-Day Namibia, Chobe & Vic Falls

Self-Drive Safari

Overview

This unforgettable journey takes you through Namibia, Botswana, and Zimbabwe, offering a blend of natural beauty, wildlife, and cultural richness. Start your adventure in Windhoek, then head to Sossusvlei's towering dunes, followed by Swakopmund, where the desert meets the sea.

Experience the dramatic landscapes of Spitzkoppe, the incredible wildlife of Etosha National Park, and the lush river systems in the Caprivi. Finally, cross into Botswana for unbelievable game viewing in Chobe before ending at the majestic Victoria Falls.
