18-Day Namibia, Delta, Chobe & Vic Falls

Self-Drive & Fly Safari

Overview

Embark on an 18-day adventure through Southern Africa, exploring the best of Namibia, Botswana, and Zimbabwe. Start in Windhoek, Namibia, with a relaxing stay at Ti Melen Boutique Guesthouse, before heading into the stunning Namib Desert at Desert Homestead Lodge. After exploring the iconic dunes of Sossusvlei, visit Swakopmund and Charlie's Guesthouse, where you can enjoy the town's charm. Experience the beauty of Spitzkoppe Lodge, surrounded by granite mountains, and then head to Etosha Safari Camp near Etosha National Park for incredible wildlife experiences.

Your journey continues at Shametu River Lodge along the Kavango River, where you can enjoy boat safaris and relax. In Botswana's Khwai Community Area, stay at Khwai Guest House for an authentic safari experience. Visit Chobe Bush Lodge in Kasane, where you'll explore Chobe National Park's wildlife, before finishing your tour at Pioneers Victoria Falls in Zimbabwe. Marvel at the breathtaking Victoria Falls and enjoy thrilling activities, rounding off an unforgettable adventure through three incredible countries.

Itinerary

Day 1-2: Ti Melen Boutique Guesthouse

Begin your adventure at Ti Melen Boutique Guesthouse in Windhoek, the capital of Namibia. This intimate guesthouse offers a peaceful and comfortable environment, making it the perfect base to start your journey. The cozy rooms and welcoming atmosphere provide a relaxing experience before setting off for your Namibian exploration.

Day 2-4: Desert Homestead Lodge

Next, head to Desert Homestead Lodge, located in the heart of the Namib Desert near Sossusvlei. This lodge offers stunning views of the desert landscape, with opportunities to explore the world-famous dunes and the surrounding wilderness. Day 4-6: Charlie's Guesthouse

After your time in the desert, travel to the coastal town of Swakopmund and stay at Charlie's Guesthouse. This charming guesthouse offers a cozy atmosphere with a touch of Swakopmund's German colonial history. Enjoy exploring the seaside town, with its beautiful beaches, unique architecture, and adventure activities like dune boarding and quad biking.

Day 7-8: Etosha Safari Camp

Head to Etosha Safari Camp, located near the southern entrance of Etosha National Park. This comfortable lodge offers easy access to the park, known for its incredible wildlife and salt pans. After an exciting day of safari drives, return to the lodge to relax and enjoy delicious meals in a welcoming environment.

Next, stay at Onguma Bush Camp, located within the Onguma Nature Reserve, bordering the eastern side of Etosha. This camp offers a perfect blend of luxury and nature, with panoramic views of the waterholes that attract a wide variety of wildlife. The intimate atmosphere and expertly guided game drives make it a highlight of your Namibian journey.
