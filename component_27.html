A bespoke luxury journey through legendary landscapes.

Welcome

Prepare for an unforgettable adventure. This exclusive journey takes you through iconic landscapes, blending adventure and ultimate relaxation at every turn.

01
WINDHOEK — TI MELEN BOUTIQUE GUESTHOUSE

Arrival in Windhoek

Experience the vibrant heart of Namibia in Windhoek, a city where modern luxury meets rich history. Settle into your boutique guesthouse and unwind after your travels. Tomorrow, your grand safari begins.

02
SOSSUSVLEI — DESERT HOMESTEAD LODGE

Arrival in Sossusvlei

Immerse yourself in the surreal beauty of Sossusvlei. Towering crimson dunes contrast starkly against blinding white salt pans. Rise early to climb the iconic Dune 45 and marvel at the hauntingly beautiful, petrified trees of Deadvlei.

03
SWAKOPMUND — CHARLIE'S GUESTHOUSE

Arrival in Swakopmund

Breathe in the fresh Atlantic breeze in Swakopmund, Namibia's premier coastal town. Sandwiched between the roaring ocean and the rolling desert, it offers both thrilling adventure activities and deep, peaceful relaxation.

04
SPITZKOPPE — SPITZKOPPEN LODGE

Arrival in Spitzkoppe

Marvel at the 'Matterhorn of Namibia', where giant bald granite peaks pierce the deep blue desert sky. Spitzkoppe offers spectacular, unpolluted stargazing and a dramatic, echoing silence that will ground your soul.

05
ETOSHA SOUTH — ETOSHA SAFARI CAMP GONDWANA COLLECTION NAMIBIA

Arrival in Etosha South

Set against the southern edge of Etosha National Park, prepare for premium wildlife encounters. Relax by your lodge's private watering hole as majestic creatures emerge from the thick bush to drink during the golden hour.

06
ONGUMA NATURE RESERVE — ONGUMA BUSH CAMP

Arrival in Onguma Nature Reserve

Enjoy absolute privacy and refined luxury at the exclusive Onguma Nature Reserve. Sip premium sundowners while safely observing lions, rhinos, and elephants right at your pristine doorstep.

07
BAGANI — SHAMETU RIVER LODGE

Arrival in Bagani

Find unprecedented tranquility in Bagani along the Caprivi Strip. Listen to the gentle flow of the Okavango River and witness brilliant, rare birdlife in this lush, tropical oasis that starkly contrasts the southern deserts.

08
KHWAI COMMUNITY AREA — KHWAI GUEST HOUSE

Arrival in Khwai Community Area

Surrender to the untamed beauty of the African wilderness. Let the rhythm of nature guide your days as you explore breathtaking landscapes, encounter iconic wildlife, and rest in absolute opulent comfort.

09
KASANE — THE CHOBE SAFARI LODGE

Arrival in Kasane

Enter Kasane, the bustling gateway to the mighty Chobe River. Prepare for unparalleled river safaris where massive elephant herds bathe in the golden afternoon glow, accompanied by pods of hippos and stealthy crocodiles.

10
VICTORIA FALLS (ZIMBABWE) — PIONEERS VICTORIA FALLS

Arrival in Victoria Falls (Zimbabwe)

Stand in awe of the 'Smoke that Thunders'. Victoria Falls is a breathtaking natural wonder of the world that provides a majestic, unforgettable, and spiritually moving conclusion to your luxury journey.

--
DEPARTURE — SAFARI CONCLUSION

Departure Bound

After a breathtaking final morning, make your way back down for your departure. Journey safely and take home an eternity of beautiful memories.
